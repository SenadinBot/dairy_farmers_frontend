Region 8 | 2nd Vice-Chair

Murray Sherk

Oxford and Waterloo

Murray Sherk, a dairy farmer from Plattsville, Ontario, was elected to the Board of Dairy Farmers of Ontario (DFO) in 2012. He represents milk producers in the counties of Oxford, and Waterloo. Mr. Sherk was elected 2nd Vice-Chair in January 2015.

Mr. Sherk previously chaired the Waterloo Dairy Producer Committee and served as a member of the Progressive Dairy Operators’ executive, as well as chairing a local church board for nine years.

He was employed as a Relationship Manager with Farm Credit Canada for 25 years working with farmers and agribusiness owners. In addition, Mr. Sherk is trained in effective teamwork, problem solving, negotiating, sales and marketing, credit and financial analysis.

Mr. Sherk chairs the Audit & Planning Committee, and the Quota Committee. He also serves on the Markets and Allocation Committee and the DFO Advisory Committee for Milk. He represents DFO on the Canadian Milk Supply Management Committee, P5 Quota Committee, and the P5 Supervisory Body. Mr. Sherk’s term as the DFO representative for Farm and Food Care Ontario will end in March 2015.

Mr. Sherk owns and operates a 100-cow dairy farm near Plattsville with the support of family and staff.
