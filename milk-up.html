Milk Up

It's time to

Up your game.

Make milk a daily part of building strong fundamentals. Protein for strong muscles. Calcium for strong bones. Carbs for energy. Confidence for the game.

Grab a glass and MilkUP.
Ambassadors

connor brown

From his days being coached by his dad in the North York Hockey League to his AHL scoring title with the Toronto Marlies to his current role with the Ottawa Senators, Connor Brown is all about building from skill to skill, from goal to win. It’s the same with your personal strength, no matter what size you are – you can’t go wrong building on the foundation that the nutrients in milk provide. And like lifelong milk-drinker Connor says, “The harder you work, the luckier you get.”

Ambassadors

Jonathan Osorio

“Greatness is the byproduct of hard work.” Toronto FC midfielder Jonathan Osorio makes scoring goals look easy, but finesse, stamina and skill don’t come overnight. They take practice, hard work, and a foundation built with the help of the essential nutrients in every glass of milk.

The power of milk

HydratE

Hello, hydration. Made up of 87% water, drinking milk throughout the day helps provide your body with the fluids it needs to hustle.

Energize

The protein in milk and chocolate milk helps enhance recovery after you work out, repair damaged tissue and promote muscle strength.

Build

Get stronger, play harder. The nine grams of protein packed into one cup of 1% milk will help your muscles be primed to grow stronger and ready to recover.
