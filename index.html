Committed to Ontario families

The Dairy Farmers of Ontario is run by real Ontario dairy farmers and farm families. We’re dedicated to excellence in the production of Canadian milk – of course! But we’re also committed to education and awareness about the nutritional benefits of Ontario dairy.
