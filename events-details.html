The Terroir Symposium is Canada's leading forum for those in the hospitality and tourism sectors. It offers unparalleled opportunities to network and connect with like minded individuals from around the globe.

May 6 - 8th, 2019
7:30am - 5:15pm
444 Yonge St. Toronto, ON M5B 2H4

The Terroir Symposium is Canada's leading forum for those in the hospitality and tourism sectors. It offers unparalleled opportunities to network and connect with like minded individuals from around the globe. It provides dozens of learning opportunities through inspirational, hands-on and educational talks, workshops and panels. It showcases the very best in innovative products relevant to the foodservice industry.
